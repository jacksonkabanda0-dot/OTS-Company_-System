About Us

🏢 Who We Are

Opportunity To Serve Company Ltd. (OTS) is a leading construction materials supplier based in Kigali, Rwanda. Founded with the mission of Building Possibilities and Serving Futures, we are committed to delivering quality construction materials to contractors, builders, and individual clients across the country.

Our company specializes in the supply and delivery of sand, cement, crushed stones, and aggregates. We operate a modern fleet and maintain high standards of quality control to ensure every delivery meets our customers' specifications.

🎯 Our Mission

To be the preferred partner for construction material supply in Rwanda by providing high-quality products, competitive pricing, and exceptional service that empowers builders to create lasting infrastructure.
